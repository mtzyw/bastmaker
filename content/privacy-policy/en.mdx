# Privacy Policy

> Last updated: July 13, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to BestMaker AI (hereinafter referred to as "we," "BestMaker AI," or "the Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy aims to clearly explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our AI creation services. By using our services, website, or products, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

When you use our AI image generation services, we may collect the following types of information:

- **Account Information**: Including your name, email address, username, avatar, and other information you provide when registering or updating your account
- **Payment Information**: If you purchase our paid services, we collect necessary payment details through secure third-party payment processors (such as Stripe)
- **Image Generation Data**: Text prompts, image inputs, generation parameters, and settings you provide for AI image generation
- **Generated Content**: AI-generated images and related metadata created through our service
- **Contact Information**: Information you provide when communicating with us via email, forms, or other means
- **Subscription Information**: Email address and preferences when you subscribe to our mailing list

### 2. Anonymous Information Collected Automatically

When you visit or use our services, we may automatically collect anonymous information:

- **Device Information**: Including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use our services, including access times, pages viewed, generation history, and interaction methods
- **Performance Data**: Information about image generation performance, processing times, and service usage patterns
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information and enhance your user experience

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Providing AI Services**: Processing your image generation requests, managing your account, storing your generated images, and delivering the core functionality of our AI image generation platform
- **Improving AI Models**: Analyzing usage patterns, optimizing image generation quality, training and improving our AI models, and developing new features
- **Service Enhancement**: Personalizing your experience, providing usage statistics, and optimizing service performance
- **Communication**: Contacting you regarding your account, service changes, new features, or related products
- **Security**: Detecting, preventing, and addressing fraud, abuse, inappropriate content generation, and security issues
- **Marketing**: Sending relevant product updates, tutorials, and promotional information (if you have opted to receive them)
- **Content Moderation**: Monitoring generated content to ensure compliance with our terms of service and applicable laws

## Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing (Stripe), cloud storage providers, AI model providers, and email services
- **AI Model Providers**: Anonymous usage data may be shared with AI model providers to improve service quality
- **Compliance and Legal Requirements**: When we believe in good faith that disclosure is required by law or to protect our rights and security or those of others
- **Content Moderation**: With content moderation services to ensure generated content complies with our policies

## Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- All payment information is processed through secure payment processors like Stripe; we do not directly store complete payment card details
- Generated images and prompts are stored securely with appropriate access controls
- We use SSL/TLS encryption to protect data transmission
- We regularly review our information collection, storage, and processing practices, including physical security measures
- AI generation data is processed in secure environments with appropriate safeguards

## Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information and improve your experience. Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help us provide a better user experience. The types of cookies we use include:

- **Necessary Cookies**: Essential for the basic functionality of the website and AI image generation
- **Preference Cookies**: Allow us to remember your settings, generation preferences, and account preferences
- **Statistical Cookies**: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website and use our AI services
- **Marketing Cookies**: Used to track visitors' activities on the website

You can control or delete cookies by changing your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website and the functionality of our AI services.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we may have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will promptly take steps to delete that information.
